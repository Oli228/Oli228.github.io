[[Erich Fromm]] -The Art of Loving(1956)

#요약

  • 사랑은 두 사람의 상호작용이 있어야만 가능하다.
    • #❤️ “Love is possible only if two persons communicate with each other from the center of their existence, hence if each one of them experiences himself from the center of his existence. Only in this “central experience” is human reality, only here is aliveness, only here is the basis for love.
  • 머릿 속에 갖고 있떤 건강한 사랑을 하기 위해 알고있어야 할 상식을 글로 풀어준 책이다.
    • 다른 사람과의 관계가 건강한 사랑은 홀로서기가 가능한 독립적인 사람이다.
    • 그리고 나르시즘이 없는 사람이다.

#핵심아이디어

  • 건강한 사랑 VS 피폐한 사랑

#인상깊은문장

#🧠 They live under the illusion that they follow their own ideas and inclinations, that they are individuals, that they have arrived at their opinions as the result of their own thinking. (p. 13.)

#🌀 In love the paradox occurs that two beings become one and yet remain two. (p.19.)

#❤️ In the sphere of material things giving means being rich. Not he who has much is rich, but he who gives much. (p.22.)

#🧠 To respect a person is not possible without knowing him. (p.27.)

#🧠 When the paralysis of masculinity is more extreme, sadism becomes the main - a perverted - substitute for masculinity. If the feminine sexuality is weakened and perverted, it is transformed into masochism, or possessiveness. (p.35.)

#❤️ Infantile love follows the principle: “I love because I am loved” . Mature love follows the principle: “I am loved because I love.”. Immature love says: “I love you because I need you. Mature love says: “I need you because I love you. (p.39.)

#🏡 In erotic love, two people who were seperate become one. In motherly love, two people who were one become seperate. The mother “must not only tolerate, she must wish and support the child’s seperation. (p.48.)

#❤️ Love, in principle, is indivisible as far as the connection between “objects” and one’s own self is concerned. (p.55.)

#❤️ If I am attached to another person because I cannot stand on my own feet, he or she may be a lifesaver, but the relationship is not one of love. Paradoxically, the ability to be alone is the condition for the ability to love. (p.103.)

#❤️ To be concentrated in relation to others means primarily to be able to listen. Most people listen to others, or even give advice, without really listening. (p.105.)

#📍 Needlessly to say that concentration must be practiced most of all by people who love each other. (p.106.)

#📍 According to what I said about the nature of love, the main condition for the achievement of love is the overcoming of one’s narcissism. (p.109.)
The narcissistic orientation is one in which one experiences as real only that which exists within oneself, but are experienced only from the viewpoint of their being useful or dangerous to one…..

#🏡 Education is identical with helping the child realize his potentialities. The opposite of education is manipulation, which is based on the absence of faith in the growth of potentialities, and on the conviction that a child will be right only if the adults put into him what is desirable and suppress what seems to be undesireable.

#나의생각/성찰

  • 나의 독서인생에 2024년 8월은 사랑의 달이었다. 사랑과 관련된 책을 여러 권 읽었는데, 에리히 프롬의 사랑의 기술이 그 중 하나였다. 
  • 가장 기억에 남는 사랑은 가족의 사랑이다. 엄마의 뱃속에서 하나의 개체로 있다가,  가족은 아이가 청소년이 되면 두 개의 개체로 분리되어 독립을 시작할 수 있게 도와주고, 결혼 후 건강한 독립으로 가정을 만들 수 있도록 도와주는 것을 의미한다고 말한다. 부모는 자녀가 독립할 수 있도록 사랑해야 한다.
  • 사랑은 인간에게 뗄 수 없는 동반자인 것 같다. 항상 충실하게 사랑하고 싶다.
